# Methods footnote — genome selection criteria

Squamate genome assemblies were retrieved from NCBI (>440 assemblies across Squamata). Assemblies were
filtered to retain reference-quality genomes using the following criteria:

1. **Reference genome assemblies only.**
2. **Assembly level:** scaffold- or chromosome-level assemblies (contig-only assemblies excluded).
3. **Contig N50:** > 5 Mb (to ensure high assembly contiguity).
4. **Genome coverage:** ≥ 30× sequencing coverage.
5. **Number of scaffolds:** < 10.
6. **Number of contigs:** < 200.
7. **Best assembly per species** retained after filtering.

This yielded **38 high-quality reference genomes** analysed in the present study (**35 unique to this study**
and **3 shared with Sharma et al. 2022**).
